Instructions

a. This form shall be completed and digitally signed by the applicant and appointed Qualified Person (QP) responsible for the project on behalf of the applicant.
b. All other documents related to the fire safety works of this project, which are required for the processing of this application, are to be produced for checking by the Registered Inspectors. However, these documents need not to be submitted together with this application unless requested by the Singapore Civil Defence Force.
c. It may take you 8 minutes to complete this form.

SCDF Submission Guide for TFP/FSC

1. Forms attachments shall follow the following filename and format:
 
  • TFP/FSC application form (SCDF-FSC-APPLN.XFD)
 
  • FSC01–BP - Certification for Fire Safety Works for SCDF Building Plan (SCDF-FSC01-CFSW-BP.XFD)
 
  • FSC01–FP - Certification for Fire Safety Works for SCDF Fire Protection Plan (SCDF-FSC01-CFSW-FP.XFD)
 
  • FSC01–MV Certification for Fire Safety Works for SCDF Mechanical Ventilation/Air-Conditioning Plan (SCDF-FSC01-CFSW-MV.XFD)
 
  • FSC01–PB (Performance based)- Certification for Fire Safety Works for Performance Based Plan (SCDF-FSC01-CFSW-PB.XFD)
 
  • RI Engagement Form (SCDF-RI-Engmt.pdf)
 
  • RI Cessation form (SCDF-RI-Cessatn.pdf)
 
  • RI Form 1 or 2 (SCDF-RI_Form_1 or 2.pdf)
 
  • RI Inspection Report (SCDF- RI_Insp_Rpt.pdf)
 
  • CoCs (Certificate of Conformity) of Regulated Fire Safety Products Listing Declared (SCDF-CoC_ <COC number>.pdf)
 
  • Delivery Orders of Regulated Fire Safety Products Listing Declared (SCDF-DO_ <COC number>.odf)
 
  • FSC02 - Certification for Regulated Fire Safety Products (SCDF-FSC02_ <product name>.pdf)
 
  • FSC03 - Certification For Lift Installation & Operation (SCDF-FSC03.pdf)
 
  • FSC04 - Certification For Fire Engine Access Road And Accessway (SCDF-FSC04.pdf)
2. The QP shall declare any used of regulated fire safety products in this project and attach CoCs and Delivery Orders. In addition, QP shall ensure that the SCDF-FSC02 are attached if any used of regulated fire safety products mark with asterisk (*) in “SCDF_PLS.xls”.
3. QP shall ensure to check all the above before making a submission in order not to cause any delay in the application.
