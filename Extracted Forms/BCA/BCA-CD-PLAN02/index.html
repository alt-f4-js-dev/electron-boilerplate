(B) Layout Plans
  1. Floor plan of HS, SS or/and Non-Shelter (NS).
  2. Section and elevation of HS, SS or/and NS.
  3. Storey height, internal clear height of shelter, thickness of shelter wall, thickness of shelter floor slab, ceiling slab and *intermediate floor slab, *RC down-hang beams, are to be clearly indicated.
*if applicable

(C) Shelter Detailed Plans
  1. Area and volume of each shelter type.
  2. Location of the 2 ventilation openings and the position of the services (e.g power points and telephony point) in the shelter.
  3. Routing and penetration of services are to be clearly indicated.
(2) Numbering Convention of Plan Type
  1. The plan type CD 01 or CS 01 shall be used for 1st time plan application. For subsequent new application of the same project such as re-application (after NOD has been issued) or amendment to approved plans application, the next running number of plan type shall be used.

    e.g 1st plan application of a project of A9999-00015-2004-CD 01 was submitted and issued with NOA or NOD, For subsequent new application, the next running number of the same project shall be plan type CD 02, CD 03 etc.
