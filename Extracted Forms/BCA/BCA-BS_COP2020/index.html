Explanatory Notes

Form BPD_BS01

A. Pre-requisites

(1) The use of welded mesh is mandated for all developments where cast in-situ slab has been adopted in the design. The minimum usage of welded mesh must be at least 65% off all cast in-situ slab area.

(2) Precast staircases shall come with tread width of 275mm or 300mm for all projects except industrial projects. For industrial projects, precast staircases shall come with tread width of 250mm, 275mm or 300mm.

(3) For the mandatory use of drywall in all residential non-landed projects, the drywall must be used as partition wall for all internal dry areas such as between bedrooms, bedroom with living room etc, with the exception of party wall, toilet wall and kitchen wall.

(4) For designs of residential non-landed projects that incorporate household shelters, it is mandatory to adopt a minimum of 65% for precast household shelters. Precast household shelters using the following standard sizes will be awarded points.

B. DfMA System

(1) The Prefabricated Prefinished Volumetric Construction (PPVC) system has to be accepted by the Building Innovation Panel (BIP) and accredited under the PPVC Manufacturer Accreditation Scheme (MAS). Please refer to BCA website for the list of accepted PPVC systems.

(2) At least 80% of the steel reinforcement for composite slab must be welded mesh.

(3) Lightweight concrete panels include autoclaved lightweight concrete (ALC) panels, autoclaved aerated concrete (AAC) panels.

(4) Precision blocks refer to lightweight concrete blocks that have precise dimensions (± 1mm dimensional tolerance) and can be laid on thin bed adhesive mortar.

(5) The use of brick wall/block wall, once used, must be indicated and shown under wall system by its wall length.

C. Innovation

(1) The following shows some examples of innovative technology which helps in achieving higher construction productivity:

  1. (i) Fully integrated system such as Prefabricated Kitchen Unit (PKU) and Prefabricated Common Toilet (PCT)
  2. (ii) Fully integrated sub-assemblies not classified in the scoring matrix
  3. (iii) Prefabricated organic components e.g. precast wavy façade
  4. (iv) High-strength / lightweight materials - high strength steel reinforcement
  5. (v) Innovative structural steel connections refer to those that do not require site welding and allow faster erection and easy on-site installation of steel members.

Please seek BCA's advice on the points to be allocated.
