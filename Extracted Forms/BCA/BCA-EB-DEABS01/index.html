Section IV Prerequisites

Section IV Prerequisites (Applicable for projects As-built Score Submission on or after 1 April 2019)

I, confirm that the following prerequisites will be complied with in the As-Built Submission.
1. Energy Consumption Monitoring*
The building's Energy Use Intensity (EUI) for the past 3 years shall be calculated and submitted. The project should also calculated the projected post retrofit EUI.
2. Air-Conditioning System Minimum Operating Efficiency*
The system efficiency shall not exceed the limits indicated in the tables as required in the prevailing code on Environmental Sustainability Measures for Existing Buildings.
3. Energy Improvement on Lighting System*
To demonstrate at least 20% improvement in the lighting power budget for common areas over the baseline as required in the prevailing code on Environmental Sustainability Measures for Existing Buildings.
4. Water Consumption Monitoring*
The building's yearly water consumption (m³/GFA(m²)) for the past 3 years shall be calculated and submitted. The project shall also compute the projected water savings based on the proposed water saving measures.
5. Permanent Instrumentation for the Measurement and Verification of Chilled Water Air Conditioning Systems*
Permanent measuring instruments for monitoring of central chilled-water system operating efficiency shall be provided as required in the prevailing code on Environmental Sustainability Measures for Existing Buildings.
6. Indoor Temperature*
The normal dry-bulb temperature setting for comfort air-conditioning shall be maintained at 23ºC and above to prevent overcooling.
7. Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) Surveillance Audit*
To commit and conduct a post-retrofit IAQ surveillance audit based on the indicative methods according to SS 554 Code of Practice for Indoor Air Quality for Air-Conditioned Buildings or demonstrate good IAQ performance according to NEA's Guidelines for Good Indoor Air Quality in Office Premises.
