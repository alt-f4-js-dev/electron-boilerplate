(1) NO OCCUPATION OF BUILDING *

The building must not be occupied before a TOP/CSC has been issued as provided for under Section12(1) of the Act.

 
(2) SITE OFFICES / STRUCTURES *

All site offices / stores / work-sheds / hoardings have been demolished and the site completely cleared of all construction materials and debris.

 
(3) PARTIAL TOP *

For partial TOP applications, all areas to which the application relates have been satisfactorily separated from those areas under construction by means of robust hoardings / fencing.

 
(4) BOUNDARY CLEARANCE *

The boundary clearance, building set backs and general layout are generally in accordance with the plans approved by the Competent Authority.

 
(5) FLOOR DIAGRAM *

For non-residential building floor diagrams stating the imposed load in accordance with the Regulation 46 have been prominently displayed on every floor.
