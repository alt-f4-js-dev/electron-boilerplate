Section III Declaration

Declaration by Supervision Qualified Person for Structural Works
I, , PE Registration No. confirm that I appointed as the supervisor responsible for the supervision of engineering works within the Road Structure Safety Zone. *
I will take reasonable steps and exercise due diligence in supervising the carrying out of the engineering works within the road structure safety zone. These works carried out within the road structure safety zone should also satisfy the “Guide to Carrying Out Engineering Works within Road Structure Safety Zone and Engineering Activity on Land adjoining Public Streets”. *
I shall exercise due diligence and reasonable care in ensuring that the response of the road structures and the adjacent ground are closely monitored during the construction stage. *
I understand that I shall submit a copy of the post-condition survey report prepared and endorsed by an independent person who has the appropriate qualifications to the Authority for record upon completion of the engineering works. I also note that any defect on the road structures due to the engineering works shall be rectified in compliance with clause 4.4 of the guide. *
I understand that the proposed works within the Road Structure Safety Zone shall not start on site unless written permission is obtained from Land Transport Authority. *
I attach the following: *
- Plans for engineering works;
- Engineering evaluation report;
- Instrumentation proposal;
- Method statement of work;
- Contingency plans / Emergency procedure;
- Pre-condition survey report;
- Certified survey plan (for underground structures only); and
- Soil investigation report
