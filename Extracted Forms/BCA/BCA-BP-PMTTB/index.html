Annex Consultation with Relevant Authorities for Technical Clearances

The applicant should consult and obtain approval and clearances required from the relevant landowners and authorities listed in the table below to enable their requirements to be incorporated in the plans before submission of the application.
List of Authorities to be consulted
a. Chief Engineer, Central Building Plan Unit, Pollution Control Department.
b. Chief Engineer, Central Building Plan Unit, Sewerage and Drainage [Water Reclamation (Network) Department, PUB], if proposal involves the use of existing or new sanitary appliances and facilities.
c. Chief Planner, URA (Development Control Group) if the period of the trade fair or exhibition exceeds a total of 90 days in a year.
d. Commissioner, Parks & Trees Regulatory Section, National Park Boards.
e. Director, Fire Safety & Shelter Department, SCDF.
f. Head URA (Car Parks Division) if public carparks are used.
g. Licensing Officer, Singapore Police Force, Public Entertainment Licensing Unit.
h. Manager, Land Transport Authority, Development & Building Control (Road & Transport).
i. Manager, Land Transport Authority, Development & Building Control (Rail).
j. Manager, Land Transport Authority, Development & Building Control (Vehicle Parking).
k. Commissioner, Building Control, Advertisement Licensing Department, Building Plan & Management Group, BCA for a licence to display any advertisement or sign.
