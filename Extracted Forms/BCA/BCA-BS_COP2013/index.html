Explanatory Notes

Form BPD_BS01
Form BPD_BS01 must be completed and submitted together with the application for approval of building plans for projects subjected to buildable design requirement. For projects with multiple blocks, please submit 1 copy of the form for every block.

For additional building blocks, select the SUMMARY sheet. Click the "Add Block" button. Click OK to confirm to add a block.

To delete a building block, click the "Delete Block" button. Enter the sheet name of the block to delete. Click OK to confirm deletion.
Structural System
(1) At least 80% of the steel reinforcement for composite slab must be welded mesh.
(2) Precast Joints are to be taken from the following guides
i. BCA's Buildable Solutions for High-Rise Residential Development
ii. BCA's Structural Precast Concrete Handbook
iii. BCA's Buildable Solutions for Landed Residential Development in Singapore
iv. HDB's Precast Pictorial Guide
(3) At least 65% of the precast joint types used in a project must come from any of the publications mentioned in (2).
(4) At least 65% of the precast component must use mechanical connection.
(5) High strength concrete of at least Grade 70 must be used for at least 5% of the total concrete volume.
(6) Self-compacting concrete must be used for at least 30% of the total concrete volume.
(7) Diaphragm wall must be adopted for at least 65% of the permanent retaining wall at basement.
Wall System
(1) Lightweight concrete panels include autoclaved lightweight concrete (ALC) panels, autoclaved aerated concrete (AAC) panels.
