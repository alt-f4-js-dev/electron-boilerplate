SCDF Submission Guide for Building Plan

  1. For the code requirements which have not been complied with or which need modification/waiver, the applications for modification or waiver of the requirements under Section 27 of the Act shall be submitted and approval granted before submission of plans.
  2. The QP has to state clearly on the application form the SCDF reference nos. of all related applications for the same project.
  3. All plans are to be drawn to standard metric scale.
  4. Scale of each drawing is to be stated clearly on plans.
  5. All plans are to be drawn on sheets of the international "A" series and all drawings are of same standard size.
  6. Indicate clearly waiver approved location and quote the waiver reference number on plan
  7. Site and key plans are to be duly coloured.
  8. Suitable sectional views incorporating the proposed system shall be provided.
  9. All floor plans and sectional views are to be complete with adequate location gridlines.
  10. All amendment and addition/alteration works are to be shown coloured except existing works.
  11. No of persons provided with means of escape is to be indicated below each floor plan description.
  12. Activities / usages of all rooms/areas/compartments are to be indicated on floor plan.
  13. Type of fire protection systems to be provided shall be indicated below each floor plan heading.
  14. Proposed change of use, if any, shall be specified clearly in the project title.
  15. A complete legend of symbols, abbreviations and/or colour scheme of fire doors & fire protection installations shall be provided on plans. All pasted portions are to be cross-signed by the QP.
  16. Provision of fire engine accessibility in accordance with current Fire Code has to be provided and shown on site plan. Designated driveways/access roads to withstand the operating load of fire engine appliances are to be hatched on plan.
  17. Fire resistance rating of fire doors, compartment walls/floors and elements of structure in accordance with the current Fire Code shall be indicated on plan.
  18. Calculations on the designed occupancy load and exit requirement per storey in FSSB-0-FSPBP02 are to be submitted (if applicable). These calculations are required even though the plan submission is for a small part of the storey.
  19. Position of external hydrant/s along public roads in the vicinity of the subject site & additional provision of internal hydrant/s, if any, within the subject site shall be indicated on site plan.
  20. Position of breeching inlet/s, wet/dry riser outlets & hydraulic hose reels, whichever is applicable, shall be indicated in red on floor plan.
  21. Detail calculations of the unprotected openings on elevations shall be submitted on FSSB-0-FSPBP02.
  22. Fire Safety Report, Fire Safety Instruction Manual and any other reports (sprinkler hydraulic, engineering smoke control calculations etc.) shall be submitted to SCDF, if required
  23. Applicable to plans that contain any prescribed fire safety measure, where prescribed fire safety measure means -

    (a) an area of refuge;
    (b) an automatic fire alarm system;
    (c) an emergency voice communication system;
    (d) a Fire Command Centre;
    (e) a fire lift or fire escape bed lift;
    (f) a fire sprinkler or water mist system;
    (g) a pressurisation system;
    (h) a rising main or breeching inlet;
    (i) a smoke control system; or
    (j) a smoke-free approach to exit staircase or smoke-free lobby.

SCDF Submission Guide for Fire Protection and Air-Conditioning/ Mechanical Ventilation Plan

  1. As for the code requirements which have not been complied with or which need modification/waiver, the applications for modification or waiver of the requirements under Section 27 have to be submitted and approval granted before submission of plans.
  2. For engineered smoke control system, the PE has to certify on all plans that the proposed smoke control system has been designed in accordance with the provisions of the accepted codes/guidelines (please state the codes/ guidelines used).
  3. The PE has to state clearly on the application form the SCDF reference nos. of all related applications for the same project.
  4. All requirements in the related BP have to be incorporated in this submission.
  5. All plans are to be drawn to standard metric scale.
  6. Indicate clearly waiver approved location and quote the waiver reference number on plan.
  7. Scale of each drawing is to be stated clearly on plans.
  8. All plans are to be drawn on sheets of the international "A" series and all drawings are of same standard size.
  9. Site and key plans are to be duly coloured.
  10. Suitable sectional views incorporating the proposed system shall be provided.
  11. All floor plans and sectional views are to be complete with adequate location gridlines.
  12. Project title is to be correctly given on all plans to reflect the actual intention of the submission.
  13. A complete legend of all symbols, abbreviations and colour scheme/patterns shall be provided on plans.
  14. For alterations and additions submission, areas for submission shall be edged out clearly on floor plans.
  15. All new installations under this submission shall be fully presented in distinctive colour scheme or patterns.
  16. All existing/previously approved installations to be deleted shall be shown in yellow dotted lines only.
  17. All existing/previously approved installations to be retained shall be outlined in neutral tints of grey or black.
  18. All intermediate broken-off points in the proposed installation layout run shall be identified with their continuity links.
  19. Calculations and reports where applicable shall be submitted.
    20. The following items are applicable to air-conditioning and mechanical ventilation system plans only:

  20. All the fire compartment walls shall be clearly edged out on the floor plans with colour scheme included in the legend.
  21. The installation air volume capacity of all respective fans and other air handling equipment shall be specified on plans.
  22. Schematic air distribution line diagram for the proposed system shall be provided.
