Instructions

  1. 1. This form is to be completed and digitally signed by the Qualified Person.
  2. 2. It may take about 5 mins to complete the form.
  3. 3. Submissions for Active Mobility Plan shall include:
    1. Architectural/Engineering Plans*,
    2. Location Plans*,
    3. Cross-section views of Architecture/Structure/Road (if applicable)*,
    4. Plans illustrating proposed signs,markings and wayfinding; and
    5. Any other plans,diagrams and illustrations of the proposal as necessary.

  6. 6. Submission categories are as follows
    1. Active Mobility Plan – Agency (i.e. NParks,PUB,JTC,HDB)
    2. Active Mobility Plan – Others (i.e. Town Council,Private development’s standalone wayfinding proposals without building alteration/erection)
      		3.
  7. 7. The submission of information for cycling/shared path or bicycle parking geo-fencing information should be submitted 2 months prior to public access for gazetting and incorporate the following information

    8. Note: if the project comprises of a road opening,this section should be submitted through the “Submission for Roads” form,to reduce the number of submission required to LTA.

  8. 7.1 Gazetting of cycling and shared path (for all entities):
    1. Traffic/street/location plan*
    2. Description of the path*
  9. 7.2 Geo-fencing of bicycle parking facilities (for Statutory Boards and Town Councils projects only):
    1. Number of lots/ racks/ size of yellow bicycle parking box (in mm and number bicycles it is designed to accommodate).
    2. Address and Exact location indicated over One Map as the base.
  10. 8. Photo of the exact location where the racks/ box will be located. Upon completion of project,the as-built plans must be submitted to LTA no later than 3 months and will be subject to inspection by LTA Officers.

    11. * Denote mandatory field
