Instructions

  1. When submission of plan is done by a Qualified Person and/or Fire Safety Engineer, it is advisable to have this application form completed, digitally signed and submitted by the Qualified Person and/or Fire Safety Engineer as the applicant(s)
  2. For particulars of the waiver items, the applicant should state with regard to the modification / waiver sought, why he considers the endorsement of the provisions of the requirements unreasonable relative to the circumstances of the case.
  3. The applicant may, if aggrieved by the decision of the Commissioner appeal in the prescribed form and manner to the Minister within 28 days of the date of notification of the decision.
  4. $160/- shall be payable per specific requirement per block/floor/shaft. Separate fee shall be charged for different block/shaft or for each design variation located at different floor of the same block/shaft. QP may wish to refer to SCDF website (https://www.scdf.gov.sg/home/fire-safety/plans-and-consultations/waiver-application) for illustrations.
  5. A letter of authorization for the Qualified Person by the owner/occupier/Management Corporation shall be provided together with the submission. The submission will be considered null and void if information related to the submission is withheld or not declared.
  6. It may take you 4 minutes to complete this form.
