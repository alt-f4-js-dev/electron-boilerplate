(d) the plot abuts a public road;

(j) such plans of the works referred to in paragraph 1 above that were lodged with the Competent Authority;
(k) the requirements of all other relevant authorities (including LTA);
(l) addition of gross floor area which does not exceed 50% of the existing and approved gross floor area of the existing landed dwelling house;
(m) removal and replacement of external walls of the existing landed dwelling house where the new walls are not more than 50% of the existing external walls;
(n) structural changes to existing floor area such as replacing or constructing new columns/beams and reconstructing existing floor slabs which are not more than 50% of the existing structural elements;
(o) changes/replacement of entire roof, regardless of increase in height as long as it does not involve an additional storey;
(p) addition of an attic provided the increase in GFA complies with criterion (l); and
(q) works that do not result in a change to the landed housing form (e.g. from semi-detached dwelling houses to detached dwelling houses, linked houses to detached dwelling houses).

(b) is not affected by any Road/Drainage proposals;
