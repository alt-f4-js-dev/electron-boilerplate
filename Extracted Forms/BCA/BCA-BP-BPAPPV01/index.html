Instructions

  1. This form is to be digitally signed by the appointed Qualified Person and/or Developer.
  2. For Design & Build project, document stating that the Builder is appointing the Qualified Person is to be attached.
  3. For waiver application BCA-BP-WVBP01.XFD (if any), to be submitted separately using BCA-BP-Waiver application folder in ESPro.
  4. For A&A Works, every application for approval of building plans involving repairs, alterations and/or additions to an existing building is required to be submitted together.
  5. From 1 July 2013, all architectural plans with 1st application for planning permission dated on or after 1 July 2013, for new developments with gross floor area (GFA) of 20,000 sq. m. and above are required to be submitted in BIM format for approval.
  6. From 1 July 2015, all architectural plans with 1st application for planning permission dated on or after 1 July 2015, for new developments with gross floor area (GFA) of more than 5000 square meters are required to be submitted in BIM Format for approval.
  7. Full plan fees for the building works of the entire development shall be payable upon submission of the first set of plans to the Commissioner Building Control for approval.
  8. This form may take 4-6 mins to complete.
