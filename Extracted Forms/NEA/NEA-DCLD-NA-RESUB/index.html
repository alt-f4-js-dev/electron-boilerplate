Declaration by Qualified Person for Structural Works

I confirm that:
Air Conditioning and Mechanical Ventilation (ACMV) equipment
1.
Locations and model types of the Air Conditioning and Mechanical Ventilation (ACMV) equipment have been indicated clearly on the submitted plans. New Condensing Units (CUs)/Variable Refrigerant Volume Systems (VRVs)/Cooling Towers (CTs) /Chillers/Generator are shown in blue. New Exhaust Air Fans (EAFs)/Fresh Air Fans (FAFs) and their direction flows are shown in magenta and green respectively. All existing and deleted equipment has been indicated in cyan and yellow respectively. *
2.
Setback distances and direction flows from the CUs/VRVs/EAFs/FAFs/ CTs/chillers/ACMV equipment to the nearest boundary line(s) of the development are indicated clearly on the submitted plans. *
3.
The CUs/VRVs/EAFs/FAFs/CTs/chillers/ACMV equipment provided are not facing neighbouring premises directly. If the ACMV systems are facing surrounding developments directly, mitigating measures (i.e. screens) have been installed for these equipment to minimise any potential disamenities to the neighbours. *
4.
The information provided (e.g. noise measurement locations, type of noise meter, extrapolation distances etc.) in the submission are in line with the requirements stated in the Guideline on Boundary Noise Limits for Air Conditioning and Mechanical Ventilation Systems in Non-industrial Buildings. *
5.
The measurement readings recorded in the submitted noise report are able to comply with the limits stated in the Guidelines. *
6.
Specifications and photographs of the CUs/VRVs/EAFs /FAFs/CTs/chillers/ACMV equipment have been provided in the submission. *
7.
A Type 1 noise meter was used for the measurement and the calibration certificate has been submitted. *
Road/Rail Traffic Noise
1.
A Noise Impact Assessment (NIA) study has been submitted for residential/noise-sensitive developments located within 70m from aboveground or at-grade Category 1 and 2 roads, MRT tracks and stations to demonstrate that with appropriate mitigation measures, the facade and indoor noise levels (Leq 1hr) of the proposed development is able to comply with 67 dBA and 57 dBA respectively under naturally ventilated conditions (with windows and sliding doors opened).
2.
For residential buildings/noise-sensitive premises (such as old age homes and schools etc.) located beside aboveground or at-grade MRT tracks/stations, the development is situated at least 35m (if front-facing) or 25m (if end-wall facing) from the MRT tracks/stations.
